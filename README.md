# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard

Analyze mobile sales data to uncover revenue trends, top brands, and regional performance using an interactive dashboard.

---

## 📊 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing mobile sales data to understand business performance.  
The dashboard helps identify sales trends, customer behavior, and high-performing products to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall mobile sales performance  
- Identify top-selling brands and models  
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends  
- Analyze regional sales distribution  
- Understand customer purchase behavior  

---

## 🚀 Features
- 📈 Total Sales & Revenue Tracking  
- 🏆 Top Brands & Best-Selling Models  
- 🌍 Region-wise Sales Analysis  
- 📅 Monthly & Yearly Trends  
- 👥 Customer Insights  
- 🎛 Interactive Filters & Slicers  

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- Power BI  
- SQL  
- Python (optional)  
- Excel / CSV  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Kaggle / Sample dataset  
- Contains:
  - Brand & model  
  - Sales amount & quantity  
  - Region  
  - Date of purchase  
  - Customer details  

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- A small number of brands contribute to most of the revenue  
- Sales peak during specific months (seasonal trends)  
- Certain regions perform significantly better  
- Mid-range phones have the highest sales volume  
- Premium phones generate higher revenue per sale
